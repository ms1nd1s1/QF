About Us

The QWABE FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization established to uplift, empower, and inspire underserved communities through access to opportunity, education, and mentorship. Our foundation believes in the transformative power of collective support and sustainable development as a means to reshape futures.

Founded with a deep commitment to social justice and economic equity, the foundation aims to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity by offering mentorship, business incubation, skills development, and scholarship programs.
