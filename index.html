Welcome to the QWABE FOUNDATION

About Us

The Qwabe Foundation is a beacon of hope, committed to empowering underprivileged youth through access to education, mentorship, and sustainable development initiatives. Our mission is to create a generation of changemakers who will build a better South Africa.

Programs

We run mentorship programs, academic support groups, scholarship drives, and entrepreneurial bootcamps that prepare young people to thrive in their chosen paths.
