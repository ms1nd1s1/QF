Get Involved

There are many ways to become part of the QWABE FOUNDATION family. You can volunteer your time, become a mentor to young entrepreneurs, contribute your skills during events, or assist with planning and logistics. We also welcome partnerships with schools, businesses, and organizations that share our passion for community upliftment.

Events

Our events are designed to educate, empower, and unite. We host entrepreneurship bootcamps, leadership seminars, youth expos, clean-up campaigns, coding days, and women’s empowerment circles. These events bring together change-makers to share skills and inspire action.

Donate

Your financial support allows us to keep our programs alive and impactful. Every contribution goes toward scholarships, training resources, venue bookings, and food support during community outreach. We accept once-off donations, monthly giving, and sponsorships. Help us grow the mission.

Contact

Have questions, feedback, or want to speak to a coordinator? Contact us via email at contact@qwabefoundation.org or phone at +27 11 123 4567. We're also available via social media. We’d love to hear from you!

Calendar

Our annual calendar highlights important campaign dates, event deadlines, national awareness days, and internal meetings. It helps members, volunteers, and partners stay aligned with the mission’s heartbeat.
